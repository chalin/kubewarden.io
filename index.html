Why Use Kubewarden

Freedom of choice

Write policies using your favorite programming language, as long as it can produce Wasm modules.

Feel at home

Policies are regular programs. Use the tools you love, reuse your skills, libraries and best practices.

Portable

Wasm modules are portable, once built they can run on any kind of architecture and Operating System.
